# Ametyst Company Overview

Ametyst is a banking platform purpose-built for the agentic economy, enabling AI agents to spend and receive money autonomously within compliant, programmable financial accounts.

## Company Background

**Founded:** 2025

**Founders:** Patrick Pinta, Michele Barberis

**Headquarters:** Italy (remote-first)

**Legal structure:** Italian innovative startup (SRL innovativa), structured as a technology provider — not a regulated financial institution

**History:** Patrick and Michele previously co-founded a peer-to-peer lending platform, gaining firsthand experience building regulated financial products. After an independent research phase focused on monetary technologies, banking infrastructure, and stablecoins, they founded Ametyst to build the native financial infrastructure layer for AI agents.

## Company Mission

**Mission:** Build the native financial infrastructure for the AI-agent economy — enabling agents to autonomously pay for and consume digital services at runtime, in a scalable, safe, and interoperable way.

**Vision:** Become the default banking and payments layer for the agentic economy in Europe, then globally — the financial operating system where agents and digital services transact.

**Values:**
- AI-first operations: all internal processes are structured for agentic workflows; Ametyst is a first-party user of the paradigm it builds for customers
- Compliance by design: European regulatory constraints are built in from day one, not retrofitted
- Open standards: built on open protocols (x402) rather than proprietary walled gardens
- Human-agent collaboration: work is designed around humans and AI agents operating as a single system
